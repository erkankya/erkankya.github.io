About Us

Dileyzi Game Studio is an independent development team providing game development, web development, and application development services. Our team consists of developers, designers, artists, and sound specialists working together to transform ideas into functional and high-quality products. We support indie developers, startups, businesses, and creative projects by delivering reliable technical solutions. Our services include Unity game development, gameplay systems, prototypes, WebGL projects, web applications, websites, mobile applications, UI/UX implementation, optimization, and technical support. We believe successful products are built through strong collaboration, clear communication, and attention to detail. Our goal is to help clients bring their ideas to life efficiently and professionally.
